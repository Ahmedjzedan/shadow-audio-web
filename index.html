Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2, 2024

Short Summary

ShadowAudio is designed with privacy as its core principle. We do not upload your audio recordings to any server. All audio is processed and stored locally on your device.

1. Data Collection & usage

Microphone Data: The app uses your device's microphone to record audio in a circular buffer. This recording happens continuously in the background if enabled.

Local Storage: All recordings are saved directly to your device's internal storage. We do not have access to these files. They are never uploaded to our servers.

2. Background Recording

To provide the "Shadow Rewind" features, the app records audio while running in the background. A persistent notification will always be displayed in your status bar while recording is active, ensuring you are always aware when the microphone is in use.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your audio data with third parties. Usage data (such as app crashes) may be collected anonymously by services like Sentry to help us improve the app stability.

4. Advertising

The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect data to provide personalized ads.
